Meter Study Participation

What is this?

This is a small instructional website for participants in Meter, a study being conducted by the Interaction Design Lab.

The purpose of this research is to design and evaluate novel measures for the self-report of pain. These measures are intended to be deployed on mobile devices as part of electronic diaries. We anticipate this research resulting in an effective measurement tool that can be used to support the daily experience of pain, as well as contributions to the fields of human-computer interaction and psychometrics.

We have developed several prototypes for such self-report measures of pain. As a participant in this stage of the research study, we will ask you to participate in a 3 week field trial of the measures.

Participation will mean installing two applications onto an Android phone. You will enroll in 2 surveys in the Ohmage application; each day of the study, you will complete each survey. Each survey consists of 4 questions; we anticipate each survey taking you 1-3 minutes to complete each day.

Set-up should take about 10 minutes. Thank you for your participation!

1. Informed consent

To get started, please review and submit this informed consent form. You can also download a copy of the form for your records (.pdf).

2. Install Meter

Please install the Meter app from the Play Store. This is an app developed by our lab.

Once installed, there is no need to set anything up. It's ready to go!

3. Install Ohmage

Please install the Ohmage app from the Play Store. Ohmage is an open-source participatory sensing app, developed by multiple academics including Deborah Estrin at Cornell University's Tech Campus.

4. Join the Meter study

Now that you have installed Ohmage, you need to join the Meter study. Open the 'projects' tab, and then tap on the plus '+' icon. Select 'Meter study' and then tap 'participate'.

5. Set up reminders

This is the final part of setup! We will set two reminders in Ohmage, one for each of the mini surveys in the Meter study. Again open the 'projects' tab, and select the 'Meter study' you just joined. In the top right-hand corner, tap on the 'bell' icon to get to Reminders.

Tap the plus '+' icon, and set up a time reminder for the Meter survey part 1/2, for any time in the morning that works for you.

Tap the plus '+' icon again to set up a time reminder for the second survey, for any time in the evening that fits your schedule.

This video shows you how:

6. Participate!

Each day of the study, please complete both surveys, one in the morning and the other in the evening. The reminders you have set up will help by providing notifications, but you can also fill out a survey from the 'Surveys' tab in Ohmage.

The study lasts three weeks. The following video shows an example of completing one of the (short!) Meter surveys. Thank you for your participation!
